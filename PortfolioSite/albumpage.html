Soundgarden

Released on March 8th, 1994

Superunknown is the fourth album from the band Soundgarden. It contains popular songs such as Spoonman, Black Hole Sun, and Fell on Black Days. The album has sold over 9 million copies worldwide.

Band Members

  • Vocals - Chris Cornell
  • Lead Guitar - Kim Thayil
  • Bass, Drums, Vocals - Ben Shepard
  • Drums - Matt Cameron
